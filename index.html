Our Vision

Many of our customers want to make significantly better products that their competitors. They want to turn product innovation and superior process efficiency to their strategic competitive advantage. They envision product cycles in the order of months rather than years. And they have chosen Keko Fabrication and Engineering Ltd. as a partner to empower such a strategic transformation.

Keko Fabrication and Engineering Ltd. is a successful, high added-value company. We have grown from our roots being registered as Kartar Singh & Hari Singh Ltd. in the early 1940s, to a high tech company for the engineering equipment, fabrication and services industry.

We are well established in agriculture products and tools, setting up new production plants. Over the last decade, we have also developed a great reputation for our commitment, zest and refine state of the art technology and services in the engineering sector.

Our team has collaborated with many of our customers in multi-year strategic transformation programs to help them adopt an integrated innovation approach involving people, systems and processes.

Most will agree that what makes us really special is our in-depth engineering knowledge, our process understanding, experience and our willingness to truly partner with our customers.

Over the years, Keko Fabrication and Engineering Ltd. has maintained a track record of profitable double-digit growth.

We are confidnet that by understanding our customers' challenges, anticipating their future needs, and responding with innovative solutions - we will continue to grow and lead our industry forward in empowering engineering innovation.

Whether by facilitating joint venture projects and consultancy agreements, or through providing expert services in various areas, we have always been a major contributor.

With over 70 years of experience in the industry, we are prepared to enter our new area of operations in the region.

As we perform our existing areas of operation, we extend our activities into new fields and territories. We look forward to yet another half century of fulfilling the ever-increasing needs of the public and private sectors in Tanzania as well as other neighboring countries
